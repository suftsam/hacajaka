PINNACLE ARTS CENTER




SILENT ART AUCTION

11th September – 25th September

The Pinnacle Arts Center is organizing a silent art auction in support of its youth mentorship program, PYMAREP.


PYMAREP is a charitable initiative by Pinnacle Arts Center, using art forms as tools to mentor and rehabilitate teens and youth from psychosocially challenged environments and communities in Ghana.

Silent Auction Hours: 10 am – 6 pm (Mon – Fri) 10 am – 3 pm (Sat)


Viewing or bidding after closing hours can be done virtually by scanning the QR Code below to view images and text your bids with the corresponding item number to 0551660077.


This is an opportunity to support the next generation of creative minds, whilst acquiring a major work by the most innovative and imaginative artist of his generation, whose works on three occasions have gone through successful sales at the bonhams auction in London.
